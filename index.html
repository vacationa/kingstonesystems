Questions you may have

What exactly does Kingstone Systems do? +

We build custom AI agents and automations for ecommerce stores that handle your customer interactions—chat, email, phone calls, order management, inventory tracking, logistics coordination, fulfillment automation, returns & exchanges, shipping questions, and customer support. Unlike off-the-shelf software, we design intelligent automation specifically for your online store, integrate with platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and your existing tools, and continuously optimize performance. Think of us as AI transformation consultants who happen to build really good automation systems for ecommerce.

How do you ensure the AI agents don't make mistakes? +

We start with a working prototype that you test before it ever interacts with real customers. Every interaction is logged and reviewed, with escalation protocols for complex situations. The AI agents are trained on your specific products, policies, and brand voice—not generic templates. We also run continuous monitoring and monthly optimization sessions to catch edge cases and improve performance.

Do I need to change my ecommerce platform? +

No. We integrate with your existing platform—Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento, or custom solutions. The AI connects via APIs to access order data, inventory, and customer information. Your team keeps using the tools they already know. No platform switching required.

Pricing depends on call volume, complexity, and integration requirements. Most ecommerce stores see systems pay for themselves within 2-4 weeks through reduced cart abandonment, improved customer satisfaction, and reduced support costs. We'll show you projected ROI before you commit—transparency is kind of our thing. Book a consultation to get specific numbers for your store.

Ecommerce stores see immediate ROI from reduced cart abandonment, faster customer response times, and 24/7 availability during international shopping hours. Yes. You get full access to call logs, performance dashboards, and our team for ongoing optimization. We provide training for your staff on monitoring and managing the AI, plus monthly check-ins to review performance and implement improvements. Support is included—not an add-on.

Absolutely. We work with you to define the tone, personality, and communication style that matches your brand. Want it professional and concise? Warm and conversational? Playful and energetic?
