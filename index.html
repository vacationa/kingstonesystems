Questions you may have

What exactly does Kingstone Systems do? +

We build custom Voice AI agents for service businesses that handle your customer interactions—phone calls, lead qualification, appointment booking, and more. Unlike off-the-shelf software, we design systems specifically for service businesses, integrate them with your existing tools, and continuously optimize performance. Think of us as AI transformation consultants who happen to build really good voice agents for service companies.

How do you ensure the AI doesn't mess up a conversation? +

We start with a working prototype that you test before it ever talks to real customers. Every conversation is logged and reviewed, with escalation protocols for complex situations. The AI is trained on your specific scenarios, FAQ, and brand voice—not generic templates. We also run continuous monitoring and monthly optimization sessions to catch edge cases and improve performance.

Do I need to change my phone system or CRM? +

No. We integrate with your existing infrastructure—whatever phone system, CRM, scheduling software, or workflow tools you currently use. The AI connects via APIs and webhooks, so your team keeps using the tools they already know. No platform switching required.

How much does this cost? +

Pricing depends on call volume, complexity, and integration requirements. Most clients see systems pay for themselves within 2-4 weeks through increased conversion rates and reduced labor costs. We'll show you projected ROI before you commit—transparency is kind of our thing. Book a consultation to get specific numbers for your situation.

What is the projected return on investment (ROI) and how fast will I see results? +

Our plumbing client saw 464% ROI in 14 days by capturing previously missed after-hours calls. Most businesses see positive ROI within the first month from improved response times, higher conversion rates, and reduced staffing needs. We calculate your specific projected ROI during the consultation based on your current call data, conversion rates, and revenue per customer.

Do you offer support and training? +

Yes. You get full access to call logs, performance dashboards, and our team for ongoing optimization. We provide training for your staff on monitoring and managing the AI, plus monthly check-ins to review performance and implement improvements. Support is included—not an add-on.

Can I customize how the AI sounds and speaks? +

Absolutely. We work with you to define the voice, tone, personality, and language patterns that match your brand. Want it professional and concise? Warm and conversational? Regional accent? We configure it to sound exactly how you want your business represented.

What if I want to go back to human-only interactions? +

You can turn it off anytime—no contracts locking you in. Your data and call logs are yours to keep. We build systems that you can eventually run in-house if you want, or scale back to specific use cases. You're never trapped with us.
