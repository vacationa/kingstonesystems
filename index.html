Questions you may have

What exactly does Kingstone Systems do? +

We build custom AI agents and automations that handle the repetitive, time-consuming parts of running a business—customer inquiries, lead qualification, appointment scheduling, follow-ups, data entry, and more. Unlike off-the-shelf software, we design systems around how your business actually works, integrate with the tools you already use, and keep improving performance over time. Think of us as an AI implementation partner who stays invested in your results.

How do you ensure the AI agents don't make mistakes? +

We start with a working prototype that you test before it ever interacts with real customers or leads. Every interaction is logged and reviewed, with clear escalation paths for anything outside the AI's scope. Agents are trained on your specific services, policies, and brand voice—not generic templates. We run ongoing monitoring and monthly optimization to keep improving accuracy over time.

Do I need to change any of my existing tools? +

No. We work with what you already have—CRMs, scheduling tools, helpdesks, communication platforms, and custom software. The AI connects via APIs and integrations so your team keeps using familiar tools without disruption. No ripping and replacing required.

How much does this cost? +

Pricing depends on the scope, complexity, and integrations involved. Most clients see systems pay for themselves within a few weeks through time saved, faster response rates, and improved conversion. We'll walk you through projected ROI before you commit—no vague promises. Book a call and we'll give you real numbers for your situation.

How quickly will I see results? +

Most clients see meaningful impact within the first few weeks—faster response times, fewer missed leads, and reduced load on their team. Positive ROI typically comes within the first month from time saved and improved conversion. We track the metrics that matter to your business so you can see exactly what's working.

Do you offer support and training? +

Yes. You get full access to call logs, performance dashboards, and our team for ongoing optimization. We provide training for your staff on monitoring and managing the AI, plus monthly check-ins to review performance and implement improvements. Support is included—not an add-on.

Can I customize how the AI agents communicate? +

Absolutely. We work with you to define the tone, personality, and communication style that matches your brand. Want it professional and concise? Warm and conversational? Playful and energetic? We configure it to communicate exactly how you want your brand represented across all channels—chat, email, and phone.

What if I want to go back to manual processes? +

You can turn it off anytime—no contracts locking you in. Your data and interaction logs are yours to keep. We build systems that you can eventually run in-house if you want, or scale back to specific use cases. You're never trapped with us.
