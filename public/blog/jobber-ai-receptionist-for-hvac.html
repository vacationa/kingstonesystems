Section 1: Jobber AI Receptionist Overview for HVAC

Jobber AI Receptionist is an AI-powered call answering feature integrated into Jobber's field service management platform. For HVAC businesses using Jobber, it provides automated call handling that integrates directly with your Jobber workflows.

1.1 What Jobber AI Receptionist Does for HVAC

Jobber AI Receptionist can:

  • Answer HVAC service calls 24/7
  • Handle basic HVAC questions (hours, services, pricing)
  • Create jobs directly in Jobber from qualified calls
  • Check technician availability in real-time
  • Route emergency calls appropriately
  • Schedule appointments based on technician availability
  • Provide basic information about HVAC services

1.2 Key Benefits for HVAC Businesses

  • Integrated Workflow: Seamless connection with Jobber platform
  • Automatic Job Creation: Qualified calls become jobs automatically
  • Real-Time Scheduling: Checks actual technician availability
  • 24/7 Availability: Never miss a call, even during peak seasons
  • Reduced Admin Time: Automates call handling and data entry

1.3 Ideal Use Cases

Jobber AI Receptionist works well for:

  • HVAC businesses already using Jobber
  • Companies with standard service offerings
  • Businesses with moderate call volumes
  • Operations with straightforward scheduling needs

Section 2: Unique Needs of HVAC Businesses

HVAC businesses have specific requirements that AI receptionist solutions must address:

2.1 Seasonal Volume Management

HVAC businesses experience dramatic seasonal fluctuations:

  • Summer (Cooling Season): 3-5x normal call volume
  • Winter (Heating Season): 2-4x normal call volume
  • Spring/Fall: Lower volume, maintenance-focused

AI receptionist must handle these spikes without degradation in service quality.

2.2 Emergency Service Handling

HVAC emergencies require:

  • Immediate recognition of urgent situations
  • 24/7 availability for emergency calls
  • Proper escalation to on-call technicians
  • Understanding of what constitutes an emergency

2.3 Technical Question Handling

HVAC customers often ask technical questions:

  • System specifications and compatibility
  • SEER ratings and efficiency questions
  • Refrigerant types and availability
  • Ductwork and ventilation questions
  • Heat pump vs. traditional system questions

AI must understand HVAC terminology and provide accurate information.

2.4 Complex Scheduling Requirements

HVAC scheduling involves:

  • Multiple technicians with different skill sets
  • Service area routing (technician location)
  • Job duration estimates (installation vs. repair)
  • Emergency slots reserved for urgent calls
  • Seasonal availability adjustments

2.5 Service Type Differentiation

HVAC businesses offer various services:

  • Emergency repairs
  • Routine maintenance
  • System installations
  • Duct cleaning
  • Indoor air quality services
  • Commercial vs. residential services

AI must understand and route these appropriately.

Section 3: Jobber Integration and Workflow

Understanding how Jobber AI Receptionist integrates with Jobber:

3.1 Native Integration Benefits

Jobber AI Receptionist's integration with Jobber provides:

  • Automatic Job Creation: Qualified calls automatically create jobs in Jobber
  • Real-Time Availability: Checks actual technician schedules
  • Customer Data Sync: Pulls customer information from Jobber
  • Service History Access: Can reference past service calls
  • Unified Platform: All operations in one system

3.2 Workflow Automation

Typical workflow:

  1. Call comes in to AI Receptionist
  2. AI qualifies the call (service type, urgency, location)
  3. AI checks technician availability in Jobber
  4. AI schedules appointment or creates job
  5. Job appears in Jobber for technician assignment
  6. Customer receives confirmation

3.3 Data Flow

Information flows between systems:

  • Caller information → Jobber contact
  • Service request → Jobber job
  • Appointment time → Jobber schedule
  • Call transcript → Jobber job notes

Section 4: Setup Process for HVAC Businesses

Step-by-step setup guide:

4.1 Prerequisites

  • Active Jobber account with field service management
  • Jobber AI Receptionist feature enabled
  • Phone number configured in Jobber
  • Technician schedules set up in Jobber
  • Service types and pricing configured

4.2 Initial Configuration

  1. Enable AI Receptionist: Activate in Jobber settings
  2. Configure Business Information: Hours, services, service areas
  3. Set Up Greeting: HVAC-specific greeting
  4. Configure Service Types: Emergency, maintenance, installation, etc.
  5. Set Routing Rules: Based on service type, urgency, location
  6. Test Integration: Verify Jobber connection works

4.3 HVAC-Specific Setup

  • Configure emergency protocols
  • Set up seasonal availability rules
  • Define service area boundaries
  • Configure technician skill matching
  • Set up service type routing
